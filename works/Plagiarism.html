Plagiarism
Packaging, Album Design

Cover design and promotional materials for the band’s album.
*The original album is "盗作(Plagiarism)" by Yorushika.

As a designer deeply inspired by Yorushika's Plagiarism — an album exploring themes of artistic creation, self-identity, and the blurred line between "originality" and "plagiarism" — I reimagined its visual identity through a fragmented, introspective lens.
